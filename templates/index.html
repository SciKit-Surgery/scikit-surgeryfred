This is the SciKit-Surgery Fiducial Registration Educational Demonstrator (FRED). SciKit-SurgeryFRED is designed for teaching and research on the registration algorithms commonly used for image guided surgery. You can find a tutorial on how to use SciKit-SurgeryFRED here.

SciKit-SurgeryFRED collects anonymous usage data to examine the relationship between registration error and treatment margins. By using SciKit-SurgeryFRED you may be contributing to future research in this important field. Click on the "Try Fred" button below if you are happy to continue.

You can get in touch via email or by our GitHub page.

You can also checkout our presentation and/or publication from SPIE Medical Imaging 2021.
